Epic Boss Fights

Igris

First Major Boss Fight

Abilities

  • Master swordsmanship
  • Enhanced strength and speed
  • Combat experience
  • Loyal to the Shadow Monarch

Outcome

Jin-Woo defeats Igris and gains him as his first shadow soldier, marking a significant turning point in his power growth.

Beru

Ant King Boss Fight

Abilities

  • Immense physical strength
  • High-speed movement
  • Regeneration abilities
  • Command over ant army

Outcome

Jin-Woo defeats Beru and gains him as a powerful shadow soldier, significantly increasing his military strength.

Jeju Island Raid

Large-Scale Boss Fight

Challenges

  • Multiple S-Rank monsters
  • Strategic coordination
  • Massive ant army
  • Environmental hazards

Outcome

Jin-Woo successfully clears the Jeju Island gate, establishing himself as one of the world's strongest hunters.
